Welcome to Blackjack!

You have managed to find one of the very best blackjack games on the internet! Or at the very least it is probably halfway decent for someone who coded it all in JavaScript during their first two weeks of a coding bootcamp. Hopefully you already know the rules of the game. We are assuming a little bit of knowlege of here!

To start the game you place a bet of either $1, $2, or $5 by hitting the big red buttons. From there you can deal and then you can either hit or stand. If a button is unavailable to you then it means it is not good time to be hitting that button! Give it a shot and let me know if you find any mistakes I can fix. Thanks for playing!
